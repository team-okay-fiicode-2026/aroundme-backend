Relationship notes

  • auth_passwords.user_id is both the primary key and foreign key, making password credentials one-to-one with a user.
  • auth_social_accounts, sessions, user_skills, and user_items are all many-to-one children of users.
  • auth_social_accounts enforces uniqueness on (provider, provider_user_id).
  • user_skills enforces uniqueness on (user_id, tag).

What this does not show

Index-only migrations such as 000003_session_indexes.sql are not drawn as tables. This page is meant to make table structure and foreign key shape easy to scan quickly.
